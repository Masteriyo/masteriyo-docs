## Course Review on Course Completion

Once the student completes the course, they can review the course by clicking on the \*Rate this course\*\* button on the top-right side.

![course-review](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/053d65de-4ab7-4461-a5a3-bd1b0b57be40)

The, a popup will appear from where the students can rate the course and write a review on that.

![course-review-popup](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/cd04827a-aea8-4089-8e24-6de6d46fe8ac)
