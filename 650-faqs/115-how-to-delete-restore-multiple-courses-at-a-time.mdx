## How to delete/restore multiple courses at a time?

You can bulk delete or restore courses in Masteriyo with just one click. Select the courses you want to delete at Masteriyo > Courses > All Courses. Once you select the courses, a popup will appear to confirm if you want to delete the courses.

![bulk-trash](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/abd07e0f-4cd1-42b8-8960-6655898aea26)

Click on **Trash** and the courses will be moved to Trash section.

Repeat similar steps to restore course from the Trash Section. Here, you can either restore the courses or permanently delete them.

![bulk-restore](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/7b3cab07-af65-4b50-a47b-eef994076712)
