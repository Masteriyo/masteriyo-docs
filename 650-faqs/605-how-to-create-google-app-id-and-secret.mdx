This documentation will help you generate Google App ID and secret required for integrating the Masteriyo Social Login add-on.

- Go to [Google Console](https://console.developers.google.com/)
- Create a new project by giving the project name

![create-project](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/26ec17e1-a0de-4acf-8e36-3d07158b536e)

After creating a project youâ€™ll be redirected to Google console dashboard.

- Click on the **Credentials** option the left side bar.

![credentials](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/3395aa58-9832-4fa3-8a72-731b062d19e6)

- Click on the **+Create Credentials** button on the top bar.
- From the dropdown, click on the **OAuth client ID**.

![create-oauth-id](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/96da7244-87d8-4081-8995-94ecfe229b1f)

- Select the **Web application** option on the radio button options.
- Enter the redirect URL given in the Masteriyo > Settings > Advanced > Social Login.

![redirect-url-google-oauth](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/7a28c2a8-1fb5-4e5b-be0f-c8f9302f1d94)

![redirect-url-authentication](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/6cbbc8da-2c2f-4a52-b33d-51024bfab5f1)

- Click on the **Create** button.

You will then get a pop-up with the App ID and secret. It is termed as Client ID and secret in the pop-up.

![app-id-and-secret-generated](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/d0c5b2ba-2272-4415-b617-b225d16254f2)

You can now use these credentials to integrate Google Login on the Masteriyo. Please note you have activated the Social Login add-on.
