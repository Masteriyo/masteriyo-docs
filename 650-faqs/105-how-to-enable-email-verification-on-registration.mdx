---
title: How to enable email verification on registration?
show_child_cards: true
excerpt: Check this out to enable the email verification feature while registering.
---

Go to the following location

        Masteriyo > Settings > Advanced > Email Verification

![email-verification](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/da417037-ec1e-4c03-8901-99e69599fd4d)

Here, you will find a toggle to enable or disable the email verification feature.

When enabled, the students registering on your site will receive a link on their email. By clicking on this link, they will be able to complete the registration process.

If disabled, the students will automatically log in to your site and will be redirected to their account page.
