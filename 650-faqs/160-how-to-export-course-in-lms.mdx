 The PDF export feature in Masteriyo LMS Pro empowers you to transform your online courses into professional, portable documents that can be accessed anywhere, anytime—even without an internet connection.

## Prerequisites:
1. [Masteriyo LMS Pro plan or higher](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/) (Plugin version 2.21.0 or above).
2. [Active installation of Masteriyo LMS](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version).

## Step 1: Access the Course Builder
In your WordPress dashboard, go to **Masteriyo > Courses**.   

![Navigate to Masteriyo Courses](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/export-single-course/navigate-to-masteriyo-courses.png)

## Step 2: Locate Your Course  
Find the course you want to export in the course list.

## Step 3: Access Course Actions  
Click on the three vertical dots (⋮) under the **Actions** column for your selected course.

![Action for Course](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/export-single-course/action-option-for-course.png)

## Step 4: Export as PDF
Select **Export as PDF** from the dropdown menu.

![Export Course as a PDF](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/export-single-course/export-course-as-pdf.png)

The PDF file will automatically download to your device's default download folder.

![Downloaded Course in a PDF](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/export-single-course/downloaded-course-in-pdf.png)

**Pro Tip**: Update your course content before exporting to ensure the PDF contains your latest information.

![PDF File of the Course Downloaded](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/export-single-course/pdf-lesson.gif)
