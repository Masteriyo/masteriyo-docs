Masteriyo addons extend the core functionality of your Learning Management System, providing additional features for course creation, user management, integrations, and more. This guide walks you through the complete process of activating both free and premium addons.

<Embed>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FhDHgK2uMzo</Embed>

## License Requirements:
- Free Addons: Available with the Masteriyo free plugin.
- Premium Addons: Require an active Masteriyo Pro license.

<Callout>
  {" "}  
  **Note:** Some add-ons require specific plugins to be active on your site first. For example, to enable the WooCommerce integration add-on, you must install and activate the WooCommerce plugin before activating the WooCommerce integration. 
  </Callout>

## Step-by-Step Activation Process of Masteriyo LMS Addons

1. Log in to your WordPress **Dashboard**.
2. Go to **Masteriyo** >**Add-ons**.

![Activate Addon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/activate-addons/navigate-to-addons-page.png)

3. Click on the toggle button to activate your desired add-ons.

![Toggle to Activate Addon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/activate-addons/enable-masteriyo-addon.gif)
