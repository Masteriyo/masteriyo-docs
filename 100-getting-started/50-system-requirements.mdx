### General Configuration Recommendations

- **WP version**: 6.0 or higher
- **PHP version**: 7.4 or higher
- **MySQL version**:  version 8.0 or higher OR MariaDB version 10.5 or higher.

### PHP Configuration Requirements

- **Post Max Size**: 64 MB
- **Memory Limit**: 256 MB
- **Time Limit**: 300
- **Max Upload Size**: 64 MB
- **WP Remote Get**: enabled
