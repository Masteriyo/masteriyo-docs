---
title: How to enable the Header Top Bar?
show_child_cards: true
excerpt: Learn basics of using LMS by Masteriyo. How to enable the Header Top Bar.
---

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > Header Top Bar** section.

- Toggle on the Enable Header Top Bar option to reveal the header top bar options.

- Click on **Publish**.

It is shown in the image below:

![Enable Header Top Bar](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183464292-14165190-5b7d-4e08-8ee3-4070128bf5f2.png)

You might not see any changes just after enabling the Header Top Bar. In that case, you will have to add the content as instructed here: [How to add content on Header Top Bar?](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/header-top-bar/add-content-in-header-top-bar)
