The Site Title and Tagline can be added and changed to best suit your website.

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Header > Site Identity** section.

- Insert the required Title and Tagline in their respective fields.

- Click on **Publish**.

To make the Site Title and Tagline visible, make sure the **Display Site Title and Tagline** option underneath the Tagline field is **checked**.
