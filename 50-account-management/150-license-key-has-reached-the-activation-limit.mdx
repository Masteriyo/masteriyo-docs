---
title: License key has reached the activation limit.
excerpt: Learn how to fix the license key that has reached the activation limit.
---

If the license activation shows "license key that has reached the activation limit" the site is reset, or backup restoration causes the issue. To resolve this, please follow the step-by-step instructions provided below:

1. Log in to your **Customer Dashboard** using your registered credentials.  
2. Navigate to the **License Management** section and deactivate the license associated with your site.  
3. Go to your website dashboard Once the license is deactivated in the customer dashboard.  
4. Reactivate the license directly from your website's dashboard.  

This process will help sync your license correctly and ensure your site functions as expected. For detailed guidance on how to log into your customer dashboard and deactivate the license, go through this link: [Customer Dashboard - Manage Site License](https://docs.masteriyo.com/account-management/customer-dashboard-manage-site-license)
