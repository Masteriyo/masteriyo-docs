Wishlist Add-on allows you to add your favorite courses and list them on your account page for later enrollment. It simply saves time searching for the courses again.


### How to Use?

![Masteriyo-LMS-Free-wishlist](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/202434753-d9e597b6-598b-4b51-8b73-e8563e166ba0.png)

1. [Masteriyo LMS](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/)
2. Enable the [Wishlist Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons)
3. Visit the Frontend Courses page. A **heart-shaped icon** indicates a wishlist.
4. **Click** the heart icon to **Wishlist** the Courses.

<Callout>
  {" "}  **Note:** When students click on the course's wishlist icon. The courses will appear on their account. 
  </Callout>
