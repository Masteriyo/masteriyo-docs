Equip your Beaver builder with Masteriyo elements. Add components like course lists and categories to any page/posts.

## Requirements

- [Beaver Builder plugin](https://www.wpbeaverbuilder.com/pricing/) to be installed and activated.
- [Beaver Integration add-on activated.](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons)

## Configuration

Once you have installed the Beaver Builder plugin and activated the Beaver Integration add-on from Masteriyo, follow the steps below.

- Go to **Beaver Builder > Templates > Add New**.
- Give the template's title and click on **Add Saves Templates**.
- Click on **Publish**.
- Click on **Launch Beaver Builder**.

![launch-beaver-builder](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/d6b5ffba-29a1-4151-a93d-4e37a8bc1c17)

- On the top-right side, click on the **+** button.
- Add a row as per your need.
- Now, click on the **+** button again and search for Masteriyo modules.

![masteriyo-modules](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/36b2b601-d799-4fb1-9253-a939779b2413)

You will have two options.

    - **Course Category**
    - **Course List**

Drag and drop the modules to the row as per your requirements. Once added, you can click on settings button on the module that has been added to the row to get the various settings for the module.

![module-settings](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/a3003de3-4269-43e3-ba9d-dd487524cc78)

Now, to show the template on your page/post, go to Beaver Builder > Templates, click on the Shortcode for the required template. This will copy the shortcode. You can add this shortcode on the required page/posts. This will then show the template you have created.

![shortcode](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/1344e0b7-1c22-433c-9bfa-f89cf87f3ccb)
