To automatically create products in WooCommerce from our course.

## Requires

- WooCommerce to be installed and activated.

## How to create product automatically in WooCommerce from our course?

1. Go to **Masteriyo > Courses**.
![Create new course!](https://jam.dev/c/49f274df-4d3f-46a7-9b68-bd2021f95b90)

2. Add a new Course. Provide the course's name and click on **Next**.
![Add new course!](https://jam.dev/c/884f606e-e885-4ef3-afd6-f2190372965b)

3. Add a new Section. Click on Add new Section and click on **Next**.
![Add new Section!](https://jam.dev/c/884f606e-e885-4ef3-afd6-f2190372965b)

4. Provide the section name. Click on **Add**.
![Provide Section name!](https://jam.dev/c/1da4d8a5-b677-4176-98b5-9b1f8f44681a)

5. Go to Pricing and select paid and Add a price for paid Course.
![Add a price for paid!](https://jam.dev/c/30ccc1d8-b265-43af-a421-45e6bf6257d3)

6. Go to WooCommerce and click on **Create Product**.
![Create a product!](https://jam.dev/c/30ccc1d8-b265-43af-a421-45e6bf6257d3)

7. Go to **Products > All Products**.
![View a product!](https://jam.dev/c/54449ab9-bec1-40d5-889f-f41db9176cc0)
