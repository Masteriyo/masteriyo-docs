The WooCommerce Integration addon lets you sell Masteriyo courses through WooCommerce. Students use the WooCommerce checkout to pay, and Masteriyo enrolls them automatically once the order is completed — no manual enrollment needed.

**Requires**

1. [Masteriyo LMS](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/) (installed and activated)
2. [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) (installed and activated)
3. [WooCommerce Integration Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons) (installed and activated)

## Setup

### Step 1 — Activate the Addon

Go to **Masteriyo > Addons** and enable **WooCommerce Integration**.

### Step 2 — Configure WooCommerce Account Settings

Go to **WooCommerce > Settings > Accounts & Privacy** and apply these settings:

- Under **Checkout** — **Uncheck** "Enable guest checkout" — this ensures every buyer must have a WordPress account so Masteriyo can enroll them.
- Under **Checkout** — **Check** "Enable log-in during checkout" — returning students can log in during checkout.
- Under **Account creation** — **Check** "During checkout" — WooCommerce will create an account for new students automatically.

![woocommerce-account-settings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/woocommerce-accounts-privacy-settings.png)

<Callout>
  If you prefer to allow guest checkout, see the [Guest Checkout](#guest-checkout) section below for the correct configuration.
</Callout>

### Step 3 — Link a Course to a WooCommerce Product

Each course you want to sell through WooCommerce must be linked to a WooCommerce product.

1. Open the course editor in **Masteriyo > Courses**.
2. Go to **Settings > Pricing** and set the course to **Paid** with a price.
3. Go to **Settings > WooCommerce**.

![wc-create-product](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/course-editor-woocommerce-create-product.png)

You have two options here:

- **Create Product** — Masteriyo automatically creates a new WooCommerce product of type **Masteriyo Course** and links it to this course.
- **Link Existing Product** — If you already have a WooCommerce product for this course, select it from the dropdown and click **Link Product**.

You can confirm the linked product under **Dashboard > Products > All Products**.

### Step 4 — Students Buy and Get Enrolled

Once a product is linked, the course shows a **Buy Now** button on the courses page. Students click it, complete the WooCommerce checkout, and are automatically enrolled as soon as the order status reaches **Completed**.

<Callout>
  WooCommerce handles the payment, checkout, and order emails. Masteriyo handles enrollment and the student learning experience.
</Callout>

## Global WooCommerce Settings

Go to **Masteriyo > Settings > Integrations > WooCommerce Integration** to configure the following options.

![masteriyo-woocommerce-integration-settings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/masteriyo-woocommerce-integration-settings.png)

### Unenrollment Status

Choose which WooCommerce order statuses automatically remove a student's enrollment. The default statuses are **Cancelled**, **Refunded**, and **Failed**. You can add or remove statuses as needed.

### Enable Add to Cart

By default, Masteriyo shows a **Buy Now** button that takes students directly to checkout. Enabling **Add to Cart** mode lets students add multiple courses to a WooCommerce cart and check out together.

When this mode is active:
- The **Buy Now** button becomes **Add to Cart**.
- After the course is added to the cart, the button changes to **View Cart**.
- You can customize both button labels using the **Label (Before Adding to Cart)** and **Label (After Adding to Cart)** fields.

<Callout>
  Add to Cart mode only applies to courses that have a linked WooCommerce product. Courses without a linked product continue to show the default Masteriyo enroll button.
</Callout>

When Add to Cart mode is active and WooCommerce orders exist for Masteriyo courses, a notice appears at the top of **Masteriyo > Orders** with a link to the WooCommerce orders screen — so you can view order details without leaving Masteriyo.

### Auto Redirect to Courses

When enabled, students are automatically redirected to the course they purchased after completing the WooCommerce checkout — instead of landing on the standard WooCommerce order confirmation page.

## Enrollment Confirmation Email

When a student's WooCommerce order is marked as **Completed**, Masteriyo sends them an enrollment confirmation email in addition to WooCommerce's standard order email. This email confirms the course the student is enrolled in.

To customize the content of this email, go to **Masteriyo > Settings > Emails**.

## Guest Checkout

If you want to allow students to check out without creating an account first, here is how to configure it correctly so enrollment still works.

Go to **WooCommerce > Settings > Accounts & Privacy** and apply these settings:

- Under **Checkout** — **Check** "Enable guest checkout" — allows students to buy without creating an account upfront.
- Under **Account creation** — **Check** "During checkout" — WooCommerce will create a WordPress account for the guest automatically, which Masteriyo uses to complete enrollment.

Once WooCommerce creates the account (either immediately at checkout or after order confirmation), Masteriyo automatically enrolls the student in their purchased courses — no action required from the student or admin.

**Admin notice:** If Masteriyo's guest checkout setting is enabled but WooCommerce is not set up to create accounts during checkout, a warning notice appears in the Masteriyo admin area. Click the link in the notice to jump directly to the WooCommerce setting that needs to be changed.

## Create a Course Directly from the WooCommerce Products List

If you already have WooCommerce products set up and want to turn one into a Masteriyo course, you can do so directly from the products list — without going into the Masteriyo course editor first.

1. Go to **Dashboard > Products > All Products**.
2. Hover over the product you want to convert.
3. Click **Create Course** in the row actions.
4. A confirmation dialog appears. Check the box to confirm the product type will change to **Masteriyo Course**, then click **Convert & Create Course**.

![wc-create-course-row-action](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/products-list-create-course-row-action.png)

A new Masteriyo course is created and automatically linked to that product. If the product is already linked to a course, the row action shows **Edit Course** instead.

## How to Unlink or Remove a WooCommerce Product from a Course

To remove the link between a WooCommerce product and a Masteriyo course, delete the WooCommerce product:

1. Go to **Dashboard > Products > All Products**.
2. Find the product linked to the course and move it to trash.
3. Open the trash and permanently delete the product.

The course is not deleted — only the WooCommerce link is removed.

## WooCommerce Subscription Support

Masteriyo integrates with [WooCommerce Subscriptions](https://woocommerce.com/document/subscriptions/) to support recurring course payments. Students pay on a schedule (weekly, monthly, etc.) and stay enrolled as long as the subscription is active.

**Additional requirement:** WooCommerce Subscriptions plugin (installed and activated).

### Setting Up a Recurring Course

1. Open the course editor, go to **Settings > Pricing**, set it to **Paid**, and enable **Recurring**. Set the recurring amount and billing interval.

2. Go to **Settings > WooCommerce** and click **Create Product**.

3. Go to **Dashboard > Products**, open the product that was just created, and on the **General** tab of the Product Data section, set the same recurring amount and billing interval as the course.

Once a student purchases the course, WooCommerce creates a subscription for them. You can view and manage all active subscriptions from **WooCommerce > Subscriptions**.

## Course Bundle WooCommerce Integration

Course bundles (a collection of multiple courses sold together) can also be sold via WooCommerce, with support for both one-time and recurring subscription purchases.

### Selling a Bundle via WooCommerce

1. Open the course bundle editor in **Masteriyo > Course Bundles**.
2. Set a price for the bundle under **Settings > Pricing**.
3. Go to **Settings > WooCommerce** and click **Create Product** (the button is only active when the bundle has a price).

### How Enrollment Works for Bundles

- After the student completes checkout, they are enrolled in all courses included in the bundle.
- On the bundle listing and single bundle pages, an **Add to Cart** button lets students add the bundle to the WooCommerce cart without a page reload.
- For recurring subscriptions, once an active subscription exists the **Add to Cart** button changes to **Explore**, taking the student directly to the bundle.
