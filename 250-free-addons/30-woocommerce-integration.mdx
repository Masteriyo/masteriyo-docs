The WooCommerce Integration addon connects your LMS to WooCommerce, letting you sell courses through any WooCommerce payment gateway. Students complete checkout through WooCommerce, and enrollment happens automatically — no manual action needed.

**Requirements**

1. [Masteriyo LMS](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/) (installed and activated)
2. [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) (installed and activated)
3. WooCommerce Integration add-on activated — go to **Masteriyo > Addons** and enable it ([how to activate addons](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons))

## Setup

### Step 1 — Link a Course to a WooCommerce Product

Each course sold through WooCommerce needs a linked WooCommerce product.

1. Go to **Masteriyo > Courses** and open the course you want to sell.
2. Go to **Settings > Pricing**, set the course to **Paid**, and enter a price.
3. Go to **Settings > WooCommerce**.

![wc-create-product](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/course-editor-woocommerce-create-product.png)

You have two options:

- **Create Product** — automatically creates a new WooCommerce product of type **Masteriyo Course** and links it to the course. The new product will appear under **Dashboard > Products > All Products**.
- **Link Existing Product** — if you already have a WooCommerce product for this course, select it from the dropdown and click **Link Product**. The product stays as-is; only the link between the course and the product is established.

### Step 2 — Students Buy and Get Enrolled

Once a product is linked, the course shows a **Buy Now** button on the courses page. Students click it, complete checkout, and are automatically enrolled when the order status reaches **Completed**.

<Callout>
  WooCommerce handles payment, checkout, and order emails. Enrollment is triggered automatically once the order status reaches **Completed**.
</Callout>

## Global WooCommerce Settings

Go to **Masteriyo > Settings > Integrations > WooCommerce Integration** to configure the following options.

![masteriyo-woocommerce-integration-settings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/masteriyo-woocommerce-integration-settings.png)

### Unenrollment Status

Choose which WooCommerce order statuses automatically unenroll a student. The defaults are **Cancelled**, **Refunded**, and **Failed**. Add or remove statuses as needed.

### Enable Add to Cart

By default, the course page shows a **Buy Now** button that sends students straight to checkout. Enabling **Add to Cart** lets students add multiple courses to the cart and buy them together.

When enabled:
- The **Buy Now** button becomes **Add to Cart**.
- After adding to the cart, the button changes to **View Cart**.
- Customize both labels using the **Label (Before Adding to Cart)** and **Label (After Adding to Cart)** fields.

<Callout>
  This setting only affects courses that have a linked WooCommerce product. Courses without one are not sold through WooCommerce and will not show an Add to Cart button regardless of this setting.
</Callout>

### Auto Redirect to Courses

When enabled, students are redirected to the course they purchased immediately after completing checkout — instead of landing on the standard WooCommerce order confirmation page.

## Enrollment Confirmation Email

When a WooCommerce order reaches **Completed**, students receive an enrollment confirmation email alongside WooCommerce's standard order email. This email confirms which course they have been enrolled in.

To customize the email content, go to **Masteriyo > Settings > Emails**.

## Guest Checkout

By default, enrollment requires every buyer to have a WordPress account. If you want to allow students to check out without creating an account upfront, configure WooCommerce as follows.

Go to **WooCommerce > Settings > Accounts & Privacy**:

- Under **Checkout** — **Check** "Enable guest checkout"
- Under **Account creation** — **Check** "During checkout" — WooCommerce creates a WordPress account for the guest automatically, which is used to complete enrollment.

![woocommerce-account-settings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/woocommerce-accounts-privacy-settings.png)

Enrollment happens once the account is created — no action needed from the student or admin.

**Admin notice:** If guest checkout is enabled here but WooCommerce is not set to create accounts during checkout, a warning appears in the admin with a direct link to the setting that needs fixing.

## Create a Course from a WooCommerce Product

If you already have WooCommerce products and want to turn one into a course, you can do it directly from the products list — no need to start from the course editor.

1. Go to **Dashboard > Products > All Products**.
2. Hover over the product.
3. Click **Create Course** in the row actions.
4. A confirmation dialog appears. Check the box confirming the product type will change to **Masteriyo Course**, then click **Convert & Create Course**.

![wc-create-course-row-action](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/products-list-create-course-row-action.png)

A new course is created in **draft** status and automatically linked to that product. Open it in **Masteriyo > Courses** to add content and publish it. If the product is already linked to a course, the row action shows **Edit Course** instead.

## Managing the Course–Product Link

You can manage the link from either side:

**From the course editor** — Go to **Masteriyo > Courses**, open the course, and go to **Settings > WooCommerce**. From here you can create a new product, link an existing one, or remove the link.

**From the WooCommerce product editor** — Open the product under **Dashboard > Products > All Products**. In the **Product data** section, set the type to **Masteriyo Course** and use the **Course** tab to select or change the linked course.

To remove the link entirely, delete the WooCommerce product. The course itself is not deleted — only the link is removed.

## WooCommerce Subscription Support [Pro]

Integrate with [WooCommerce Subscriptions](https://woocommerce.com/document/subscriptions/) to charge students on a recurring schedule. Students stay enrolled as long as the subscription is active.

**Additional requirement:** [WooCommerce Subscriptions](https://woocommerce.com/document/subscriptions/) plugin (installed and activated).

### Setting Up a Recurring Course

1. Open the course editor, go to **Settings > Pricing**, set the course to **Paid**, and enable **Recurring**. Set the recurring amount and billing interval.

![course-editor-recurring-pricing](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/course-editor-recurring-pricing.png)

2. Go to **Settings > WooCommerce** and click **Create Product**.

3. Go to **Dashboard > Products**, open the product that was just created, and on the **General** tab of the Product Data section, set the same recurring amount and billing interval as the course.

![woocommerce-subscription-product-general-tab](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/woocommerce-subscription-product-general-tab.png)

Once a student purchases the course, WooCommerce creates a subscription for them. View and manage all active subscriptions from **WooCommerce > Subscriptions**.

## Course Bundle WooCommerce Integration [Pro]

Course bundles — collections of multiple courses sold together — can be sold through WooCommerce with support for both one-time and recurring subscription purchases.

### Selling a Bundle via WooCommerce

1. Open the bundle editor in **Masteriyo > Course Bundles**.
2. In the right sidebar, set the **Pricing Option** to **One Time** or **Recurring** and enter a price.
3. Click **Create Product** under the **Create as a Product** option in the same sidebar.

![course-bundle-editor-woocommerce-tab](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/woocommerce/course-bundle-editor-woocommerce-tab.png)

Once linked, the bundle listing and single bundle pages show a **Buy Now** button by default. If [Add to Cart](#enable-add-to-cart) is enabled in the global settings, the button becomes **Add to Cart** and adds the bundle to the cart without a page reload. For recurring subscriptions, once an active subscription exists, the button changes to **Explore** — taking the student directly to the bundle.
