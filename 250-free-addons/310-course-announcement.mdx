The Course Announcements add-on provides a streamlined way to share updates and information with students, fostering effective communication in online courses.

**Requires**

1. [Masteriyo Plugins](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/)
2. [Course Announcement Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons)

**Setup and Configuration**

Once you have installed and activated the Course Announcements add-on, go to the following location.

        Masteriyo > Announcements

Here, you can view the previously made announcements or create a new once. For this tutorial, we will learn how to create a new announcement. 

![add-new-announcements](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/37899106-0c17-4706-8a17-f4f41912ab8b)

Click on the **Add New Announcement** button on the top-right. 

![add-new-announcements-details](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/a8938d97-3460-4b75-ad2c-5def6528ace4)

Provide the announcement title and descriptions. Select the course you want to announce for. Click on **Publish**. 

Now, you can view this announcement from the course's Learn Page. Go to the course archive page and view the respective course. On the top-right, you will see an announcement icon with the above created announcement details. 

![course-announcement-frontend](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/c72f0bb0-0da9-4fec-abcf-85b8e9c2f721)
