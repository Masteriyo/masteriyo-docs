This add-on allows teachers to share upcoming courses and content with their users.

## Installation

- Activate the [Courses Coming Soon add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons)

## Configuration

Once you have activated this add-on, go to **Masteriyo > Course > Edit/create a course**. Now, follow the steps below.

- Go to **Settings > Course Coming Soon**

![course-coming-soon](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/d17e9938-3ef0-4d1d-b3c0-0ab047e3486c)

- Enable _Course Coming Soon_ option from the toggle button.
- Set the **Enrollment Date**
- **Hide Meta Data** - This option hides the meta data of the course.
- **Hide Date Text** - This option hides the content showing the course will be available at given date.
