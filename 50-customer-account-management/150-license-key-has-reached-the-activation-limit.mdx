---
title: License key has reached the activation limit.
excerpt: Learn how to fix the license key that has reached the activation limit.
---

If you see the message "license key that has reached the activation limit," it may be because the site was reset or a backup was restored. To fix this issue, please follow the step-by-step instructions below:

1. Log in to your [Customer Dashboard](https://docs.masteriyo.com/customer-account-management/customer-dashboard-manage-site-license#how-to-log-in-to-the-customer-dashboard) using your email used during Masteriyo Subscription purchase.  
2. Navigate to the [License Management](https://docs.masteriyo.com/customer-account-management/customer-dashboard-manage-site-license#manage-site-license) section and deactivate the license associated with your site.  
3. Go to your website dashboard Once the license is deactivated in the customer dashboard.  
4. Reactivate the license directly from your website's dashboard.  

This process will help sync your license correctly and ensure your site functions as expected. For detailed guidance on how to log into your customer dashboard and deactivate the license, go through this link: [Customer Dashboard - Manage Site License](https://docs.masteriyo.com/customer-account-management/customer-dashboard-manage-site-license)
