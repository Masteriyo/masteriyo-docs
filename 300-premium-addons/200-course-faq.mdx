This feature will come in handy if you want to address frequently asked questions in your courses. Also, this add-on introduces a new FAQ tab on your course page.


## Prerequisites:
1. [Masteriyo LMS Basic plan or higher](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version).  
2. Active [installation of Masteriyo LMS](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version).
2. [Course FAQ Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons).

## Configuration 
Once you have installed the plugin and activated the addon, go to **Masteriyo**>**Courses**>**Edit**.

![activating-course-faq-with-masteriyo](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/189636513-1cb9ce80-de32-4e0a-8ae0-a9e13b0615e3.png)

1. Click on **Settings**>**FAQ**.
2. Enable the **Show FAQ**.
3. Click on **Add New FAQ**.
4. Add the question and answer in the **Title** and **Content** tab.
5. Click **Update**.

![displaying-course-faq](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/189636572-3607b015-29a0-46f7-a51c-89e0a8852ad6.png)
