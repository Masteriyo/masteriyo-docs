This feature will come in handy if you want to address frequently asked questions in your courses. Also, this add-on introduces a new FAQ tab on your course page.


### How to Use?

![activating-course-faq-with-masteriyo](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/189636513-1cb9ce80-de32-4e0a-8ae0-a9e13b0615e3.png)


1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Course FAQ Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/install-masteriyo-premium-addons)
3. Go to **Masteriyo**->**Courses**->**Edit**
4. Click on **Settings** ->**FAQ**
5. Enable the **Show FAQ**
6. Click on **Add New FAQ**
7. Add the question and answer in the **Title** and **Content** tab
8. Click **Update**
