This add-ons helps you to bundle related courses and sell them as a package.

## Requires

1. [Masteriyo Pro Plugins](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/)
2. [Course Bundle add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons)

## Configuration

### Create/add a new bundle

Once you have activated the Course Bundle add-on, go to the following location.

        Masteriyo > Course Bundles

On the top-right side of the screen, click on the **+ Add New Bundle** button.

![add-new-bundle-button](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/7bf7a74e-83de-4091-aeb3-6c0d8a773df2)

Now, you will get to the page where you can create the bundle.

![create-new-bundle](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/37a4b923-4c0f-45fe-8dc1-4fe07eaa8fd4)

- **Course Bundle Name**: Enter the name of the bundle you are creating.
- \*_Course Bundle Description_: Provide appropriate description for the bundle making sure the students understand what they are purchasing.
- **Select Courses**: From here, you can select the courses that you want to put in the bundle. The courses will appear as a nice thumbnail when you select them.

![select-courses-for-bundle](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/04774587-3908-4bc6-9571-9f23cd5dfc90)

- **Bundle Highlights**: List out the highlights of the bundle that makes it stand-out from the rest.
- **Featured Image**: Select an image to appear as the featured image for the bundle.
- **Pricing Options**: From this settings, you can choose the pricing option for the bundle from the available options.

  - **Free**: If you select this, the bundle will be available for free.
  - **One Time**: If you select this option, the students will have to pay once when they purchase the bundle and they will own it forever.
  - **Recurring**: Select this option if you want the students to be a subscriber of the bundle which have to renewed in the allocated time period. You can set the price and time period for the recurring payment.

  ![pricing-option-recurring](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/7ff8f6ee-b210-4fb4-948a-1152cfbdf6fa)

Once you have entered all of the required details for the bundle, click on the **Publish** button on the end. Or, if you are in the middle of the configuration and want to save it for later, you can click on the **Save to Draft** option.

Your bundle is now created.

### Display the Bundle

After activating the Course Bundle add-on, Masteriyo automatically create a page called **Course Bundle**. The bundles you create will all be showcased in this page.

Or, you can use the following shortcode on the desired page to show the course bundles.

[masteriyo_course_bundles]

![course-bundle-page](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/e5fbb4bc-84cd-4452-92a0-f0d99bae4140)

The users can buy the bundle by clicking on the **Buy Now** button.
