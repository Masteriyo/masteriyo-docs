This add-on helps you to bundle related courses and sell them as a package.

## Prerequisites:

1. [Masteriyo LMS Pro plan or higher](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/).
2. [Course Bundle add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons).

## Configuration

### Create/add a new bundle

Once you have activated the Course Bundle add-on, go to the following location.        

<Callout>
  {" "} 
  **Masteriyo > Course Bundles** {" "}
</Callout>

On the top-right side of the screen, click on the **+ Add New Bundle** button.

![navigate-to-course-bundle](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d73b99ae-c6be-42e3-a512-966c6795827d)

Now, it'll take you to the page where you can create the bundle.

![course-bundle-settings](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bda95757-f91b-40b0-b858-8e0cdfd77078)

- **Course Bundle Name**: Enter the name of the bundle you are creating.
- **Course Bundle Description**: Provide an appropriate description for the bundle, making sure the students understand what they are purchasing.
- **Select Courses**: From here, you can select the courses that you want to put in the bundle. The courses will appear as a nice thumbnail when you select them.

![select-course-for-course-bundle](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc3082c8-96f9-424b-82e3-2d9d5f5c45f6)

- **Bundle Highlights**: List out the highlights of the bundle that makes it stand out from the rest.
- **Featured Image**: Select an image to appear as the featured image for the bundle.        
- **Featured Video**: You can also set a video as a prominent display element for course bundles. Masteriyo LMS lets you add both external and self-hosted videos.     

![choose-video-source](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/04aa4692-4cc0-48b2-afe1-855dbec0ab12)

 - For self-hosted video, upload video files to your LMS media library and use them.        
 - For external video, you can choose the video source, such as YouTube, Vimeo, or any external URL.


![add-video-source-for-featured-video](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/91e48007-9e31-49c3-af16-3c75f1d84598)

- **Pricing Options**: From these settings, you can choose the pricing option for the bundle from the available options.

  - **Free**: If you select this, the bundle will be available for free.
  - **One Time**: If you select this option, the students will have to pay once when they purchase the bundle, and they will own it forever.
  - **Recurring**: Select this option if you want the students to be a subscribers of the bundle, which has to be renewed in the allocated time period. You can set the price and time period for the recurring payment.

 ![pricing-option-for-course-bundle](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5a2d4526-f7e2-4052-b69a-f83042fca9ba)

Once you have entered all of the required details for the bundle, click on the **Publish** button at the end. Or, if you are in the middle of the configuration and want to save it for later, you can click on the **Save to Draft** option.

Your bundle is now created.

### Display the Bundle

After activating the Course Bundle add-on, Masteriyo automatically creates a page called **Course Bundle**. The bundles you create will all be showcased on this page.

Or, you can use the following shortcode on the desired page to show the course bundles.


         [masteriyo_course_bundles]
 

![course-bundle-page](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c8405ecc-635d-4220-bf3f-3f79578fc70f)

Users can buy the bundle by clicking the **Buy Now** button. If you have added a featured image or video to the bundle, it will appear in your course bundle, similar to the image below:

![featured-video-example](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5405f4a0-5f0d-4261-a373-aed698fe941a)
