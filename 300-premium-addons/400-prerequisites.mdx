Enabling the Prerequisite feature requires students to complete one or more courses before enrolling in a particular course.

## Setup
1. Install [Masteriyo LMS Pro plan or higher](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Prerequisites Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons) 

## How to Use?

![add-prerequisites-course-with-masteriyo](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/189649360-835d246b-d21c-4cbc-8e24-8cc240c3b6fa.png)

1. Go to your  **Dashboard** and navigate **Mastyeriyo**-> **Courses**.
2. **Select** and **Edit** the course on which you want to add a prerequisite course.
3. Navigate **Settings**->**Prerequisites**.
4. Click on the toggle button to activate prerequisites.
5. Add the prerequisite courses.
6. Click on the **Update**.
