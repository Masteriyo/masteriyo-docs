This feature lets you use your brand instead of the Masteriyo plugin. With this feature, you can hide the Masteriyo plugin's images and name and replace them with your own.

### How to Use?

![enabling-white-label-in-masteriyo](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/195074601-9b6c3398-8f44-4609-af42-75c04929aa32.png)

1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [White Label Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/activate-masteriyo-premium-addons)
3. Go to your **Dashboard**, navigate **Masyeriyo**->**Settings**->**Advanced**
4. Click on the **White Label**
5. Add your Brand name in the **Title** section
6. Upload your Brand **Logo** and **Active** **Inactive Menu Icon**
7. Click on **Update**
