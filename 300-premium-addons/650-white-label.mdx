This feature lets you use your brand instead of the Masteriyo plugin. With this feature, you can hide the Masteriyo plugin's images and name and replace them with your own.

## Prerequisites: 
1. Install [Masteriyo LMS Elite plan](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version).
2. Activate the [White Label Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons).

## Configuration
1. Go to your **Dashboard**, navigate **Masyeriyo**->**Settings**->**Advanced**.
2. Click on the **White Label**.
3. Add your Brand name in the **Title** section.
4. Upload your Brand **Logo** and **Active** **Inactive Menu Icon**.
5. Click on **Update**.
