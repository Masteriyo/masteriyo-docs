Wishlist addon Allows you to add your favorite courses and list them on your account page for later enrollment. It simply saves time searching for the courses again.


### How to Use?

![Masteriyo-LMS-Free-wishlist](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/202434753-d9e597b6-598b-4b51-8b73-e8563e166ba0.png)

1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Enable the [Wishlist Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/install-masteriyo-premium-addons)
3. Visit the Frontend Courses page. A **heart-shaped icon** indicates a wishlist.
4. **Click** the heart icon to **Wishlist** the Courses.

<Callout> **Note:** When students click on the course's wishlist icon. The courses will appear on their account. </Callout>
