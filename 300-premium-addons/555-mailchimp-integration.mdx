Seamlessly integrate Mailchimp with Masteriyo LMS for efficient and automated email marketing within your learning management system.

## Setup and Configuration

- Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
- Activate the [MailChimp Integration add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/activate-masteriyo-premium-addons)

### Create a MailChimp appropriate

- Log in to your [MailChimp Account](https://login.mailchimp.com/)
- Click on profile image. 
- Go to profile.

![mailchimp-profile](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/2010d0a4-0209-4912-b83b-7fd6157a01be)

- Go to Extras > API.

![mailchimp-api](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/1bd83b5e-5b14-4319-88f1-23c5701228c9)

- Scroll down and click on **Create A Key**.

![mailchimp-create-api](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/ed69782e-bde4-4f3d-9df0-117826804461)

Now, copy the created key into a clipboard. 

### Integrating MailChimp API key with Masteriyo

Once you have generated the MailChimp API, follow the steps below.

- Go to **Masteriyo > Settings > Advanced > MailChimp Integration**

![mailchimp-integration-settings](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/a57c4cab-6243-41de-9fc0-f71a1bf3533e)

- Enable Forced Subscription.
- Enter the API key generated above. 
- Click on **Save Settings**. 

You synch the Groups and Lists you have created, click on Sync on the Clear Groups and List Cache. 

![mailchimp-sync-group-lists](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/27adfecb-1fda-4c8c-a6a9-ff7ab0b5e212)
