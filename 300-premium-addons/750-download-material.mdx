Enabling this feature allows you to add unlimited lesson materials and gives students quick access to download or preview these materials.

### How to Use?

![enabling-download-material-in-masteriyo](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/195080532-85a1674b-2fd4-4f67-a24e-2886da4b440f.png)

1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Course Attachments Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/activate-masteriyo-premium-addons)
3. Go to your **Dashboard**, navigate **Masteriyo**->**Courses**
4. Click on **Builder** option from the top 
5. Select the **Lesson** where you want to add course material
6. Upload documents on **Download Materials**
7. Click the **Update**
