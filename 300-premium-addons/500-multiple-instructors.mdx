With the Multiple Instructor feature, you can allow more than one instructor to take control of the course content.

## Prerequisites: 
1. Install [Masteriyo LMS Pro plan or higher](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Multiple Instructors Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons)

![multiple-instructor](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/189648343-b7046b89-6d86-4ff8-ab1c-6daeacf9a2eb.png)

## Configuration
1. Go to your  **Dashboard** and navigate **Mastyeriyo**-> **Courses**
2. **Select** and **Edit** the course on which you want to add multiple instructors
3. Click on **Settings**->**General**
4. Add instructor names in **Additional Instructors**
5. Click on **Update**
