Social login via Facebook and Google adds a layer of convenience by allowing users to authenticate with their existing social media accounts, streamlining the login process.

## Prerequisites:

1.[Masteriyo LMS Pro plan or higher](https://masteriyo.com/pricing/).  
2.[Masteriyo LMS installed and activated](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version).  
3.[Activate the Social Login Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/activate-masteriyo-addons).

## Configuration

Once you have installed and activated this add-on, go to the following location.

        Masteriyo > Settings > Advanced > Social Login

![social-login-settings](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/c2df363c-3e7a-461d-81ce-c2dd5663c8c5)

### Google Login

To enable Google Login in your login forms, you will need a Google Client ID and a Secret Key. 

        [Generate Google Client ID and Secret Key](https://console.cloud.google.com/getting-started?supportedpurview=project)

While creating these credentials, you will need to add a redirect URL. Copy the redirect URL given in the Masteriyo settings and use it on Google.

![google-redirect-url](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/4cc7d6d3-c99c-4161-b6a8-1a6165522525)

### Facebook Login

To enable Facebook Login in your login forms, you will need the Facebook App ID and App Secret. 

        [Generate Facebook App ID and App Secret](https://developers.facebook.com/apps/?show_reminder=true)

While creating these credentials, you will need to add a redirect URL. Copy the redirect URL given in the Masteriyo settings and use it on Facebook.

![facebook-redirect-url](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/15fb8383-0978-460b-b1d0-25445fd9b4a3)

Once you have set up these keys, you can now log in to your site via Google or Facebook.

![social-login-frontend](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/1d0ad4a9-92bb-443e-8d25-2497a0f2b7e3)
