The "Social Share" addon by Masteriyo adds social sharing functionality to the single course page.

## Setup

1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Gradebook Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/activate-masteriyo-premium-addons)

![activate-social-share](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/c46414ac-e14b-4298-8a3f-f9e07d3bb16c)

## Configuration

Once you have activate the Social Share add-on, navigate to the following location.

        Masteriyo > Settings > Single Course Page > Social Share

![social-share-settings-main](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/251855c0-f1d7-49fe-93b6-9b2db9cf59fb)
