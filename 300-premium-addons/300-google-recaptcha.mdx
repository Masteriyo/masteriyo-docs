You can easily integrate Google reCAPTCHA into your website to add an extra layer of security.

### How to Use?

![adding-recaptcha-in-masteriyo](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/24367265/189649663-dc3d7fd6-406b-4c3a-a711-d39f0b8aefb8.png)

1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Google reCAPTCHA Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/activate-masteriyo-premium-addons)

> After activating the add-on, visit https://www.google.com/recaptcha/, generate and copy the **Site Key** and **Secret Key** then select the type of **reCAPTCHA** you want to display on your website. 
 
3. Go to dashboard, navigate **Masteriyo**-> **Settings**
4. Click on **Advanced** -> **reCAPTCHA**
5. Select the reCAPTCHA version that you selected earlier
6. Incert the **Site Key** and the **Secret Key** under the **State the Site** & **Secret keys**
7. Select the forms where you want to display reCAPTCHA
8. Click on **Save Settings**
