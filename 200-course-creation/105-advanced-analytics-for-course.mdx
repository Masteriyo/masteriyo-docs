## Advanced Analytics for Courses

![masteriyo-courses-analytics](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/eccf5458-fc6f-416a-a0f6-92162dbf9674)

In the **Masteriyo > Courses**, you can find the **Reports** button on the ACTIONS column. This button will provide you with advanced analytics for the selected course.

![advanced-analytics-dashboard](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/44cb74e7-ddc1-44c5-938b-7afdd169daea)

### Student's Progress

To check the progress of the enrolled students, scroll to the Enrolled Students section. Here, you will find the list of the students enrolled to the respective course. For each students, you can click on the **Progress** button to check and modify their progress.

![enrolled-students-progress](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/8ee29ed8-509d-407a-8365-ada797412374)
