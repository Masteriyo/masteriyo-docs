# How to change the style of the Meta Tags?

With the eLearning theme, you can change the Meta style for your site as per your needs.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Content > Meta** section.

- Choose any style from Meta Style.

- Click on **Publish**.
