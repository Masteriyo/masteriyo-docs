# How to manage Sidebar of the Single Post?

With the eLearning theme, you can change the sidebar style of a single post on your site per your needs.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Sidebar** section.

- Choose any style from the title and content.

- Click on **Publish**.
