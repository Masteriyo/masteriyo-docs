# How to manage Sidebar of the Single Post?

With the eLearning theme, you can change the Sidear style of the Single Post for your site as per your needs.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Content > Sidebar** section.

- Choose any style from Title and Content.

- Click on **Publish**.
