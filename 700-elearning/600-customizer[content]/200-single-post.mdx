This theme provides you with the following options under the Single Post section:

## Post Elements

### To change the order of Post Content on the Single Post page:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Single Post** section.

- Use drag-and-drop in the Single Post Content option to give higher or lower priority to the items.

- Click on **Publish**.


```
Additional information: Clicking on the "Eye" icon will hide the content in the display window.
```

## Meta

### To change the order of meta tags on a single post page:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Single Post** section.

- Use drag-and-drop in the Meta Tags Order option to give higher or lower priority to the items.

- Click on **Publish**.

```
Additional information: Clicking on the "Eye" icon will hide the content in the display window.
```
