# How to enable the Header Top Bar?

- From your WordPress Dashboard, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > Header Top Bar**.
- Toggle on the Enable Header Top Bar option to reveal the header top bar options.
- Click on **Publish**.

You might not see any changes just after enabling the Header Top Bar. In that case, you will have to add the content as instructed here: How to add content on Header Top Bar?
