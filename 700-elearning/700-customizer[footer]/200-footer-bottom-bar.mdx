# How to manage Footer Bottom Bar?

    Location: Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Bottom Bar

eLearning allows you to customize the Footer Bottom Bar section using various options explained below:

## Style

There are two styles available in the Footer Bottom Bar. They are Left & Right align and another is center aligned. Choosing the first style will show Left Content as left align and Right Content as right align. Choosing the second style will show the Left Content above and Right Content below it with center align in the footer bottom bar.

## Left Content and Right Content

### How to add Content on the Footer Bottom Bar?

This theme allows you to add a variety of content including Text/HTML, Menu, Widget, etc on the Left and Right side of the Footer Bottom Area.

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, navigate to the **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Bottom Bar** section.

- Select the type of content from the drop-down list under the Left Content and Right Content options.

- Click on **Publish**.

The available options under the Left Content/Right Content are:

- **None**: Show an empty section.
= **Text/HTML**: Show text or HTML elements. Use the text editor below to enter the content.
- **Menu**: Show a menu. Use the dropdown list below to select the menu.
- **Widget**: Display a Widget. Go back to Appearance > Widgets, and add the required widgets under Footer Bottom Bar Left Sidebar/Right Sidebar.

## Colors

- **Background**: Select the color of the background in the Footer Bottom Bar.

- **Text**: Select the color of the text in the Footer Bottom Bar.

- **Link**: Select the color of the link in the Footer Bottom Bar.
