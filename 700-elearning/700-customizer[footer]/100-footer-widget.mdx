# How to manage Footer Widgets?

    Location: Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Widgets

eLearning also allows you to customize the Footer Widgets area using the options explained below:

## Enable Footer Widgets

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Widgets** section.

- Toggle On the Enable Footer Widgets .

- Click on **Publish**.

It is shown in the image below:

![Enable Footer Widget](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183585549-700b21d9-884c-4cd0-b35f-10ab69a77a8a.gif)


## Hide Widget Title

WIth eLearning, you can choose to show or hide the widget title from the Footer Widget area.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Widgets** section.

- Switch on the Hide Widgets Title option.

- Click on **Publish**.

It is shown in the image below:

## Footer Widget Style

Follow the steps as below:

- From your WordPress Dashboard, go to **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Widgets** section.
- Under the Footer Widgets Style option, choose the Style such as one column, two columns, three columns or four columns for footer widgets.

You have the following options under Style:

- **First option**: single column layout.
- **Second option**: 2 column layout.
- **Third option**: 3 column layout.
- **Fourth option**: 4 column layout.

![Footer Widgets Style](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183586548-4ddbba48-9d79-4877-95f5-dcb48b0d8474.png)

## Colors

- Background: You can change the background color and image of the Footer Widgets Area with eLearning.

Just follow the steps shown below:

   - From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Widgets** section.

   - Under the **Background option**s, choose the Background Color or Background Image for the Footer Widgets Area.

   - Click on **Publish**.

- **Widget Title**: Select the color of the titles of the Footer Widgets.
- **Widget Content**: Select the color of the titles of the Footer Widgets Content.
- **Widget Link Item**: Select the color of the titles of the Footer Widgets link.

![Footer Widget Colors](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183588516-7215de51-ef21-4254-add8-d7b12306231e.gi

## Border Top: 

Use Border Top to select the size of the border above the Footer Widget area. Choose the Border Top Color for changing the color of border above footer widgets.
 - **Size**: Select the size of the border above the Footer Widget area.
 - **Color**: Choose the Border Top Color for changing the color of border above footer widgets.

## List Item Border Bottom: 
- **Size**: Choose the size of the border under the items in the Footer Widgets.
- **Color**: Select the color of the border under the item in the Footer Widgets.
