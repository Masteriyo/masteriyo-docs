# How to make the Transparent Header?

You can easily make the header of your site transparent if you have the eLearning theme.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > Transparent Header** section.

- Toggle the Enable Transparent Header option. After enabling, two available options will appear.

  - **Enable on 404, Search and Archive Pages**: This option allows you to enable 404, Search, and Archive Pages in the header.
  
  - **Enable on Front Page (Latest Posts)**:  This option allows you to display the latest posts on the front page of the header.
