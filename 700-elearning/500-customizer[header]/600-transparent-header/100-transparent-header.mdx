# How to make the Transparent Header?

You can easily make header of your site transparent if you have the eLearning theme.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > Transparent Header**.

- Toggle the Enable Transparent Header option on. After enablling, two available options will be appreared.

  - **Enable on 404, Search and Archive Pages**: This option allows to enable 404, Search and Archive Pages in the header.
  
  - **Enable on Front Page (Latest Posts)**:  This option allows to display Latest posts on the front page of the header.
