# How to enable the Header Top Bar?

- From your WordPress Dashboard, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > Header Top Bar**.
- Toggle on the Enable Header Top Bar option to reveal the header top bar options.
- Click on **Publish**.

It is shown in the image below:

![Enable Header Top Bar](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183464292-14165190-5b7d-4e08-8ee3-4070128bf5f2.png)

You might not see any changes just after enabling the Header Top Bar. In that case, you will have to add the content as instructed here: [How to add content on Header Top Bar?](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/blob/elearning/700-elearning/500-customizer%5Bheader%5D/300-header-top-bar/200-add-content-in-header-top-bar.mdx)
