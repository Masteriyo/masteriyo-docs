You can modify the color of the Site Title as well as the Site Tagline in the eLearning theme.

- From your WordPress Dashboard, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Header > Site Identity** section.

- Choose the color of the Site Title as well as the Site Tagline as per your requirement.

- Click on **Publish**.
