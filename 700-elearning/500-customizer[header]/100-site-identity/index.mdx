eLearning theme allows you to manage site title, logo, tagline and others in Site identity section. 

- From Customize section, go to the **Header > Site Identity**.
- In Site identity option, you will see  available options
  - [Logo](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/site-identity/site-logo)
  - [Retina Logo](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/site-identity/retina-logo)
  - [Site Icon](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/site-identity/site-icon)
  - [Site Title and Site Tagline](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/site-identity/site-title-tagline)
  - Colors
    - [Header Text Color](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/site-identity/header-text-color) 
  - Typography
    - [Site Title](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/site-identity/typography)
    - [Site Tagline](https://docs.masteriyo.com/elearning/customizer[header]/site-identity/typography)
