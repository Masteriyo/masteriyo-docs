## How to manage Background of the site? 

eLearning theme allows you to manage the background in the site both inside and outside the container.  

- From Customize section, go to the **Global > Background** from your **WordPress Dashboard**.

- In the Background option, you will see two available options:

   - **Inside Container** 

     - ***Background Color***: It manages to add background color inside the container.
     
      ![Background Color-Inside Container](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183378795-94c901d3-ca65-40fe-a852-e99547a0b231.png)

     - ***Background Image***: It manages to add background image inside the container. 
     
     ![Background Image-Inside Container](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183380243-a1975630-0f35-4972-99fd-a12995d37e7e.png)


   - **Outside  Container**
   
     - ***Background Color***: It manages to add background color outside the container.
     
      ![Background Color-Outside Container](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183380999-187609c6-05e9-484f-940f-42ec9fd3b93d.png)

     - ***Background Image***: It manages to add background image outside the container.   
     
      ![Background Image-Outside Container](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183381592-96cbd007-6949-409e-a8ab-394c93b32f5e.png)
