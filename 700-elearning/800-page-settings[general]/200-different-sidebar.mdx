# How to show a different sidebar on a different page?

With the eLearning Theme, you can show different sidebar on a specific page. Just go through the following steps.

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Pages > All Pages** section.

- Now choose the required **Page** that you want to customize.

- At the bottom of the page, check for the **Page Settings** option.

- Under the Page Settings option, go to the **General** option.

- Under the **Sidebar option**, user the dropdown list to choose which sidebar content to show.
  Available Lists are
- Default
  - Sidebar Right
  - Sidebar Left
  - Footer One
  - Footer Two
  - Footer three
  - Footer four

     Note: If you have chosen the Left Sidebar in the Layout option and you chose the Right Sidebar in the Sidebar option, then the content of the Right Sidebar will be shown in the Left Sidebar.
