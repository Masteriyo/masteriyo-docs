# How to remove the Content Margin from the page?

With the eLearning theme, you can remove the Content Margin for specific pages.

Just follow the steps shown below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Pages > All Pages** section.

- Choose the required **Page** you want to customize.

- At the bottom of the page, check for the **Page Settings** option.

- Under the Page Settings option, go to the **General** option.

- Under the **Remove content margin** option, **check** the checkbox to remove the **Content Margin**.
