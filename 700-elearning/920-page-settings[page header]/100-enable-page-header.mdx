# How to enable/disable the Page Header of an individual page?

With the eLearning theme, you can remove the Page Header in a specific page. Just follow these steps to do so:

Just follow these steps:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Pages > All Pages** section.

- Now choose the required **Page** you want to customize.

- Below the editor, go to the **Page Settings** option.

- Under the **Page Header** option, toogle the Enable Page Header checkbox to disable the Page Header.

    Note: After following these steps, your heading will be removed if you have selected Page Header in the Page Title option of the Customizer. Select Content Area instead and the heading will be shown in the content.
