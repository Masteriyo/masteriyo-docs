# How to change the Logo of an individual page?

eLearning also allows you to change the Logo of individual pages.

Just follow these steps to do so:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Pages > All Pages** section.

- Now choose the required **Page** that you want to customize.

- At the bottom of the page, check for the **Page Settings** option.

- Under the Page Settings option, go to the **Header** option.

- Find the **Logo** option here and click on **Upload Logo** to select the logo for the page.
