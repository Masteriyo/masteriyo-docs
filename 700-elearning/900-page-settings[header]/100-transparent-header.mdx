# How to make the Header of an individual page Transparent?

You can easily make the Header of any specific page transparent using eLearning.

Just follow these steps:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Pages > All Pages** section.

- Now choose the required **Page** you want to customize.

- At the bottom of the page, check for the **Page Settings** option.

- Under the Page Settings option, go to the **Header** option.

- On the **Enable Transparent Header** option, make sure that the checkbox is checked
