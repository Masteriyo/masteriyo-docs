# Can I change the Footer Copyright Information in the theme?

Yes, you can. eLearning allows you to change the default footer copyright information and add your own content in its place. It is quite simple. Just follow these steps:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Bottom Bar**.

- Look for the field under the **Text/HTML for Left Content** and replace the default copyright content with your own.

- Click on **Publish**.
