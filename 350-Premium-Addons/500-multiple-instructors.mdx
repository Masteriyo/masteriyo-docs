With the Multiple Instructor feature, you can allow more than one instructor to take control of the course content.

### How to Use?

1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Multiple Instructors Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/install-masteriyo-premium-addons)
3. Go to your  **Dashboard** and navigate **Mastyeriyo**-> **Courses**
4. **Select** and **Edit** the course on which you want to add multiple instructors
5. Click on **Settings**->**General**
6. Add instructor names in **Additional Instructors**
7. Click on **Update**
