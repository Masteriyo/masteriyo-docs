Enabling the Prerequisite feature requires students to complete one or more courses before enrolling in a particular course.


### How to Use?


1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Prerequisites Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/install-masteriyo-premium-addons)
3. Go to your  **Dashboard** and navigate **Mastyeriyo**-> **Courses**
4. **Select** and **Edit** the course on which you want to add a prerequisite courses
5. Navigate **Settings**->**Prerequisites**
6. Click on the toggle button to activate prerequisites
7. Add the prerequisite courses
8. Click on the **Update**
