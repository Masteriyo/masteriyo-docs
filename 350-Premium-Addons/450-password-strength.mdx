Password Strength allows users to have a strong password with a combination of numbers, capital letters, and unique symbols while signing up.


### How to Use?


1. Install [Masteriyo Pro](https://docs.masteriyo.com/getting-started/installation#installing-masteriyo-lms-pro-version)
2. Activate the [Password Strength Add-on](https://docs.masteriyo.com/premium-addons/install-masteriyo-premium-addons)
3. Go to your **Dashboard**, navigate to **Masteriyo**> **Settings**
4. Click on the **Advanced**->**Password Strength**
5. Click on the **Save Settings**
