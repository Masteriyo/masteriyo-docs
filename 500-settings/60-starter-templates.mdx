Masteriyo LMS introduces Starter Templates - a collection of professionally designed, ready-to-use website templates specifically crafted for online learning platforms. These templates provide a quick and efficient way to launch your LMS website with modern, conversion-optimized designs.

<Callout>Location: Masteriyo > Settings > Templates</Callout>

![Starter Templates Page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/starter-templates/navigate-to-starter-templates.png)

After visiting the **Starter Templates** page, you can browse through the available template collection. Currently, you can explore six different templates.

## Using Starter Templates

You can hover over the templates, which gives you two options: Preview and Import.

![Preview or Import Starter Templates](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/starter-templates/preview-or-import-starter-templates.png)

### Preview Templates

- Click the Preview button on any template to see what the template will look like.
- Navigate through different sections to see the whole design.
- Test responsiveness by resizing your browser window.

### Import Templates

- Select your preferred template.
- Click the Import button and then continue after reading the pop-up message

![Read and proceed importing](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/starter-templates/hit-continue-to-proceed-importing.png)

- Wait for the import process to complete.

![Importing Demo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/starter-templates/wait-while-demo-importing.png)

- Your website will automatically apply the new template design.

![Demo Import Successful](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/starter-templates/demo-importing-successful.png)

## Template Features

### Pre-built Sections

- **Hero Areas**: Compelling headlines with call-to-action buttons.
- **Course Grids**: Organized course displays with filtering options.
- **Testimonials**: Student success stories and reviews.
- **Contact Forms**: Lead generation and inquiry forms.

## Design Elements

- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Modern Typography**: Professional fonts and text hierarchy
- **Color Schemes**: Carefully selected color palettes for each niche
- **Image Placeholders**: High-quality stock photos included
- **Button Styles**: Conversion-optimized call-to-action buttons

## Customization Options

After Import
Once imported, templates can be customized through:

- **WordPress Customizer**: Theme options and basic styling.
- **Page Builders**: Compatible with popular builders like Elementor.
- **Masteriyo Settings**: LMS-specific configurations.
- **Media Library**: Replace placeholder images with your content.

Stay updated by checking the Starter Templates section regularly for new additions and improvements.
