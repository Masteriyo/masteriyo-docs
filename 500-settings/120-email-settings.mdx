## Emails

![email-settings](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/abd3db12-9f11-4180-b349-a99472a3d3ac)

This settings is to enable/disable the email sent to different roles like admin, instructor and students. You can toggle the tabs on the left to enable/disable the emails as per the roles.

### To admin

![to-admin](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/63836abc-5c39-4464-9293-727c10747a64)

Here, you can enable/disable various email notification as per you need. Also, there is a settings icon beside every toggle button for each of the notifications. Click on this icon to modify the email content.

![email-content](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/effd35f3-1340-471f-97d8-5b1c07e7fa4c)

        Just like the admin notification, you can also set the instructor and student notifications.
