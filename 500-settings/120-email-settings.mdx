## Emails

![email-settings](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/abd3db12-9f11-4180-b349-a99472a3d3ac)

This settings is to enable/disable the email sent to different roles like admin, instructor and students. You can toggle the tabs on the left to enable/disable the emails as per the roles.

### To admin

![to-admin](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/63836abc-5c39-4464-9293-727c10747a64)

Here, you can enable/disable various email notification as per you need.

Also, there is a settings icon beside every toggle button for each of the notifications. Click on this icon to modify the email content.

![customize-email-content](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/598a9683-b3f1-484f-b074-0604cfd04031)

Here, you can customize the following.

- **To Address** - Enter the recipient's email address to send notification. Use smart tag: {admin_email}. Separate multiple recipients with comma.
- **From Name** - Enter the name you want to appear as the sender of the email.
- **From Address** - Enter the email address from which you want to send the email.
- **Reply to Name** - Enter the name to display as the reply-to name.
- **Reply to Address** - Enter the email address to receive the replies of this email.
- **Subject** - Give a subject to your email.
- **Content** - Write the email content. Here, you can find the list of all the available smart tags from the **Add Smart Tags**.
  Just like the admin notification, you can also set the instructor and student notifications.
