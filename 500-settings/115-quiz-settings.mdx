## Quiz

### General

![quiz-settings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/quiz-settings/quiz-settings.png)

- **Quiz access for guest users**: You can choose to either show the quiz in the default way for all the users, or choose read-only view for the guest users, or disable the access.
- **Auto Submission Behaviour**: Choose between auto submit and auto abandon.
- **Grade Calculation**: Select the method you want to use to calculate the student's final grade.

### Display

![quiz-display](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/main/assets/images/quiz-settings/quiz-display-settings.png)

- **Quiz Complete Button**: Display the quiz complete button only if the user passes the quiz.
- **Allow students to give feedback**: Enable this option to allow the students to give quiz feedback.
- **Enable question navigation**: Toggle to show one question per page for quiz in paginated format. 

#### Quiz Review or Student Feedback

When you enable the **' Allow students to give feedback'** option, the student who complete the quiz will be able to leave a feedback on the quiz report.

To leave a review, complete any quiz available and at last when you see the report, you will see a button on the bottom-right to leave a feedback/review.

![leave-a-review](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/29951c47-7717-4d14-8d3d-2ed88a3fd55a)

Once you click on the 'Leave a Review' button, a pop-up wll open where you can write your review.

![leave-a-review-popup](https://github.com/Masteriyo/masteriyo-docs/assets/75772539/25d02d4d-1593-480c-8b24-8541013ab055)

Now, as an admin/instructor, you can view these reviews from **Masteriyo > Quiz Attempts > Quiz Review**.

### Styling

![quiz-style](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/af746482-0bba-422d-aa49-effcfa3dfd73)

- **Questions per page** - Set the amount of the questions to appear per page.
